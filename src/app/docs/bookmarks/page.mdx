# Bookmarks

Bookmarks are your personal curation tool. You can bookmark almost anything in RGAP: a specific Grant, a Researcher, or a University.

## How to use Bookmarks

Look for the **Ribbon Icon** on cards and profiles. It looks like this:

* **Click to Save:** The icon turns solid black. The item is now in your library.
* **Click to Remove:** The icon reverts to an outline. The item is removed.

## Adding Notes

The most powerful feature of bookmarks is the ability to add context. 

1.  Go to your **Bookmarks Dashboard** via the sidebar.
2.  Find the item you saved.
3.  Click **"Add Note"**.

This is useful for teams or students who are scouting for opportunities. You can write notes like *"Application deadline is Nov 1st"* or *"This researcher's work aligns with our Q3 goals."*
