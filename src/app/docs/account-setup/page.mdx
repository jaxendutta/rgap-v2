# Account Management

Creating an account on RGAP is free and unlocks personalized features like Bookmarks and Search History.

## Creating an Account

1. Click the **Sign In** icon in the sidebar.
2. Select the **Register** tab.
3. Enter your full name, email address, and a secure password.
4. Click **Create Account**.

> **Important:** You will receive a verification email. You must click the link in that email before you can fully access your account features.

## Password Security

We take security seriously. All passwords are encrypted using **bcrypt** before being stored in our database. We never see your raw password.

### Resetting your Password
If you forget your password:
1. Go to the Login page.
2. Click the **Forgot Password?** link.
3. Enter your email address to receive a secure reset link.
